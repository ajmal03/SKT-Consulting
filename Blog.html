The Elephant in the Office

Unfiltered truths about leadership, culture, and the corporate circus.

Let's be real: most corporate advice is either sugar-coated, recycled, or straight-up batata. The blog is none of that. Here, I write about what leaders don't say out loud, what teams pretend not to notice, and what companies quietly sweep under the carpet until it eats them. If you're ready to face the elephant in your office, you're in the right place.
