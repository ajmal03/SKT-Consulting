My Services

What I Do - and Do Well

With more than 44 years in the field, I offer more than advice—I offer perspective, clarity, and action. My approach challenges assumptions, identifies blind spots, and empowers leaders and organizations to navigate today's complex world. Here's what you can expect from my services:

1

Executive Coaching

For leaders who want to be seen as smart, robust, sharper, and think bigger.

I coach senior executives who are ready to face un-processed permanency. Whether you're preparing for a C-suite role, navigating a major transition, or seeking to enhance your leadership impact, my coaching provides the insights and strategies you need to excel.

Common themes:
  • Executive Presence and gravitas
  • Strategic communications
  • Decision-making under pressure
  • Managing difficult teams
  • Transitioning to C-suite/new role
2

Leadership and Strategy Consulting

For organizations seeking bold thinking and clearer direction.

I work with organizations ranging from startups to large corporations who speak to me to unpack complex challenges, refine their vision, define their strategy, and build strong teams. I am comfortable speaking to different organizational levels and across industries.

Typical engagements:
  • Strategy unraveling and refresh
  • Vision and mission refinement
  • Culture diagnostics and transformation
  • Leadership team diagnostics
  • Business planning assistance
3

Workshops andacilitation

For teams who need more traction and less distraction.

I design and facilitate dynamic workshops that spark honest dialogue, challenge assumptions, and generate actionable outcomes. Whether you need to align a new team, resolve conflict, or develop specific skills, I create an engaging and productive environment that responds to your team's needs.

Popular topics:
  • Strategic Thinking and Planning
  • Coaching vs. Managing
  • Leading with Empathy
  • People Development and Performance
  • Conflict Resolution in the Workplace
  • Assessments and Behavioral Insights
4

Keynote Speaking and Thought Leadership

For anyone who needs to hear the truth, straight up.

I bring real-world insights, humor, and a direct, no-nonsense approach to every speaking engagement. My keynotes are designed to provoke thought, challenge the status quo, and inspire action, leaving audiences with practical takeaways.

Sample keynotes:
  • The Elephant in the Office: Why We Avoid the Truth and What to Do About It
  • When Your "Why" isn't Enough: The Limits of Purpose-Driven Leadership
  • Bold Leadership in a Batata World
  • What Millennials Taught Me About Corporate Life
